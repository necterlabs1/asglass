OUR PROJECTS

Work that We’ve done for the past 20 years

Sound Proof Glass

Block out noise pollution and create a peaceful oasis in your home or office. With up to 90% noise reduction, you can enjoy peace and quiet, even in the most noisy environments.

Spider Toughened Glass Fitting

Spider toughened glass fitting: A stylish and secure way to hold glass panels in place. These fittings are made from stainless steel and are available in a variety of sizes and finishes. They are the perfect way to add a touch of elegance to your home or office.

Shower Cubicle

A shower cubicle is a structure made of glass or acrylic panels that separates the shower area from the rest of the bathroom. It can help to contain water and provide a more private showering experience.

Glass Staircase

A stunning and modern way to add a touch of elegance to your home. These stairs are made from tempered glass, which is strong and durable. They also let in natural light, making your home feel brighter and more open.
